# **App Name**: SavoHealth

## Core Features:

- User and Doctor Authentication: Secure login for both users and doctors, requiring name, phone number, age, address, photo, and password (for returning users).
- Initial Health Profile Setup: Collect user's height, weight, and lifestyle information upon first login to calculate and store their BMI in personal history.
- Personal Health History: Securely store and manage user's health data, including BMI, medications, and previous treatments.
- AI Symptom Checker: An AI tool that analyzes user-reported symptoms to suggest potential cures and assess the severity, recommending doctor consultation or hospitalization when necessary.
- Doctor Discovery: Display a list of doctors specializing in conditions suggested by the AI symptom checker, with reviews and ratings to help users choose the most suitable professional.
- Online Doctor Consultation Booking: Allow users to book and attend online consultations with doctors directly through the app.
- Pharmacy Integration: Enable users to order prescribed medications through the app and receive home delivery.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Light blue (#A0D2EB), representing health and tranquility.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E5F1F7), providing a clean and calm backdrop.
- Accent color: Soft green (#BCE29E), suggesting growth, healing, and trustworthiness.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif offering a modern yet accessible feel suitable for both.
- Use clear and professional icons representing each feature, such as a stethoscope for doctor consultations or a pill for pharmacy.
- A clean and intuitive layout with easy-to-navigate menus and sections for each feature (Personal History, AI Help, Doctor Consultation, etc.).
- Subtle animations to acknowledge user actions and guide them through the app's processes, ensuring a smooth and engaging experience.
